Open Standards Drive Innovation

Podcast creators need the freedom to create, distribute, and monetize their content however they want.

Podcast Standards Project was established in 2022 by a consortium of independent, prominent players in the podcast space with the primary objective of protecting the open nature of podcasting enabled by the decentralized distribution of RSS.

The Podcast Standards Project (PSP) is a grassroots coalition working to establish modern, open standards for the podcast industry. The PSP Standards Spec is our first initiative centered around establishing a baseline standard for podcast RSS feeds and basic ground rules for anyone building technology for the podcast space.

Services that build or ingest RSS feeds should support the same standard set of elements, as proposed in our spec. This allows podcast hosting companies and listening applications to build in the same direction, knowing the standard element support will exist on both sides. To this end, we ask all podcast hosting companies and listening app makers to fully support the core standards before adding product-specific innovations.
